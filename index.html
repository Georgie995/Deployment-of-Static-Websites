Hobbies

Playing Football

As a child I played football in the park almost every afternoon after school.

I remember the excitement of scoring a goal and celebrating with my friends until it got dark.

Drawing and Sketching

I used to spend quiet weekends filling notebooks with cartoon characters and imaginary worlds.

One of my favourite memories is showing my drawings to my family and proudly sticking them on the fridge.

Toys

Remote-Control Car

My remote-control car was one of my most treasured toys; I raced it around the living room until the batteries died.

I remember building small obstacle courses and timing how fast I could drive it around the track.

Building Blocks

I loved stacking my colourful blocks into towers taller than I was and then knocking them down.

A clear memory is spending rainy afternoons inside, designing little cities out of blocks on the carpet.
